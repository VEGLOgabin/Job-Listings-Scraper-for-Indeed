Job Summary

The main responsibility of a Data Analysis Assistant is to support the Data Analysis team and assist in collecting, processing and analyzing data to help the company make informed business decisions. This position requires a strong sensitivity to data and certain analytical and reporting skills.

  • Minimum Qualification : HND
  • Experience Level : Entry level
  • Experience Length : 1 year

Job Description/Requirements

Responsibilities:

  • Collect and organize various types of data related to the business.
  • Use Excel, SQL, or other data analysis tools to clean and process data.
  • Assist in analyzing and interpreting data trends, generating data reports and visualization charts.
  • Support the work of senior data analysts and assist them in in-depth data analysis.
  • Regularly update and maintain data management systems.
  • Monitor and analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) to provide insights to support business decisions.
  • Assist in market research and customer behavior analysis.
  • Ensure the accuracy and integrity of data and maintain data confidentiality.


Requirements:

  • Have a basic understanding of data analysis and be proficient in using tools such as Excel and Google Sheets.
  • Have good communication and teamwork skills
  • Have basic data processing and statistical analysis skills.
  • Have problem-solving skills and a strong willingness to learn.
  • Bachelor's degree in related fields or equivalent work experience.


Those who have a certain understanding of programming languages such as SQL, Python, and R will be given priority.
